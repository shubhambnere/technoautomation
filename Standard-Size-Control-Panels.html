Standard Size Control Panels

At Techno Automation, we specialize in manufacturing standard size control panels that are designed to meet the common requirements of various industries. Our standard size control panels are carefully engineered to provide reliable control and protection for electrical systems, ensuring smooth operations and enhanced safety.

Applications of Standard Size Control Panels

Our standard size control panels find applications across various industries, including:

  • Manufacturing and Industrial Automation
  • Power Generation and Distribution
  • Water and Wastewater Treatment
  • Oil and Gas
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Food and Beverage
  • And many more.
  • Size Options
  • Quality Construction
  • Safety & Compliance
  • Standardized Components
  • Customizable Configurations
  • Easy Installation & Maintenance

We offer a range of standard sizes to accommodate different applications and installation spaces. Our panels are available in various dimensions to suit your specific needs.

The available sizes are:

  • 1000 mm   x   1200 mm   x   250 mm
  • 800 mm   x   600 mm   x   250 mm
  • 500 mm   x   400 mm   x   210 mm
  • 1200 mm   x   1400 mm   x   300 mm

Our control panels are built using high-quality materials and components to ensure durability and longevity. We follow rigorous quality control processes to guarantee that every panel meets industry standards.

While our standard size control panels come with predefined specifications, we understand that each application may have unique requirements. Therefore, we offer customization options to tailor the panel’s configuration to your specific needs.

We utilize standardized components from reputable manufacturers to ensure compatibility and easy maintenance. This allows for streamlined integration with existing systems and simplified troubleshooting.

Our standard size control panels are designed with a strong focus on safety. They incorporate appropriate safety features, such as circuit protection devices, grounding systems, and clearly labeled indicators. Additionally, our panels comply with relevant industry regulations and standards.

Our panels are designed for hassle-free installation and maintenance. Clear documentation and labeling facilitate smooth installation processes, while user-friendly interfaces and accessible components simplify maintenance and troubleshooting.
