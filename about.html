What we do?

The Making of a Legacy: Our Story So Far

At Techno Automation, we bring a wealth of experience and success to the field of electrical control panel manufacturing. Our team has a strong foundation in the industry, with a proven track record of excellence. As a subsidiary of Techno Mac Systems, a reputable manufacturer of industrial washing machines that has been thriving for over 12 years, we have expanded our expertise to meet the growing demand for high-quality electrical control panels

Techno Mac Systems has earned a stellar reputation for its commitment to innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction in the manufacturing of industrial washing machines. Building upon this legacy, Techno Automation, is now poised to deliver the same level of excellence in the electrical control panel sector.

With our deep understanding of industrial systems, combined with our passion for precision engineering and advanced technologies, we are dedicated to designing and manufacturing top-of-the-line electrical control panels that meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our focus on quality, efficiency, and safety ensures that our panels provide reliable control and protection for a wide range of applications.

As we embark on this new chapter under Techno Automation, we remain firmly committed to upholding the core values that have made Techno Mac Systems successful: integrity, customer-centricity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. By leveraging our expertise and industry insights, we aim to set new benchmarks in electrical control panel manufacturing and become a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable and efficient control solutions.

We invite you to explore our range of products and services and discover how Techno Automation can add value to your operations. Our knowledgeable team is ready to provide personalized solutions, technical support, and exceptional customer service to ensure your satisfaction every step of the way.
